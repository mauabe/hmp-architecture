About Heidi M. Puskar Architecture

Specialized in small commercial buildings and residential projects.

Heidi was born and raised in Helsinki, Finland. She graduated from University of Hawaii, and obtained an Architecure degree from SCI-ARC, Los Angeles. Heidi has a California architect’s license since 2000 and carries omissions and errors policy.

Her architecture office is equiped to handle the whole design process, builts and drafting, and works with regular structural engineers for any size project. Her office takes care of the full complicated permit process for residential services for the City of Los Angeles Building and Safety: Green, LID, Title-24, as well as for all surrounding cities — from Santa Barbara & Kern Counties to Orange and Riverside Counties.
